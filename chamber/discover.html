About Teandu

Teandu is a vibrant community in the Greater Accra Region of Ghana, known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful landscapes, and thriving local businesses. Our chamber of commerce is proud to showcase the attractions and businesses that make our area a wonderful place to visit, live, and work.
